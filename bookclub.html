Community Review

The Woman in Me

Im not giving this 10 stars because it was amazing and the best piece of literature out there. I’m giving it 10 stars because the bravery it took to put her story on paper, for all to read, is amazing. I always wondered what happened to her and to read it, breaks my heart. I’m so glad that she is free and I hope she is able to enjoy life again! I’m proud of Britney and am honored to have read this.

―R. Phillips

10/10 stars

Anna's Monthly Pick

Hello Beautiful (Oprah's Book Club): A Novel by Ann Napolitano

Anna’s local book club has chosen Ann Napolitano’s Hello Beautiful for their last meeting of the year and holiday gathering with a book gift exchange. Hello Beautiful is a best selling family drama about four sisters coping with hardship, an exquisite homage to the timeless classic, Little Women.
